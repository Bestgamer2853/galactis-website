# Company Logos Directory

Place company logo image files here. Supported formats: PNG, SVG, JPG

## Required Logo Files

To fix the logo display issues, please add the following logo files:

- `icici-bank.png` or `icici-bank.svg`
- `hdfc-bank.png` or `hdfc-bank.svg`
- `infosys.png` or `infosys.svg`
- `tcs.png` or `tcs.svg`
- `apollo-hospitals.png` or `apollo-hospitals.svg`
- `tvs-motors.png` or `tvs-motors.svg`
- `airtel.png` or `airtel.svg`
- `ashok-leyland.png` or `ashok-leyland.svg`
- `corpay.png` or `corpay.svg`

## Logo Requirements

- **Format**: PNG (transparent background) or SVG preferred
- **Size**: Minimum 256x256px for PNG, scalable for SVG
- **Quality**: High resolution for crisp display
- **Aspect Ratio**: Maintain original logo proportions

Once logos are added, the system will automatically use them instead of external APIs.
